Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this website?

To search for product ingredients, select a brand from the drop-down menu and type the product name. To search for all products carried by a brand, simply select a brand (no need to type in a product).

The brand/product I'm looking for doesn't show up.

Your brand/product may not be in our database if it is not recognized from the USDA, or is a more recent product released after 2018.

Where did all the product ingredient information come from? Is it trustworthy?

The information comes from the US Department of Agriculutre.
